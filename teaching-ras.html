Research Associates

About the Research Associate Program

Research Associates at the Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco work closely with PhD economists on cutting-edge research spanning macroeconomics, labor economics, international economics, finance, and monetary policy. The position provides excellent preparation for PhD programs in economics, with many former RAs going on to top graduate programs and receiving prestigious fellowships.

Research Associates contribute to academic papers, policy analysis, and data collection while developing skills in econometric analysis, economic modeling, and research communication.
